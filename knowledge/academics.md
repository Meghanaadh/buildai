# Academics

## Notes
- Operating Systems notes are available in the Academics > Notes section.
- Department faculty upload weekly subject resources.

## Exam Schedule
- Mid-semester and end-semester schedules are published by the exam cell.
- Students should check the exam timetable section every Monday.

## Course Registration
- Course registration opens at the beginning of each semester.
- Students must complete registration before the announced deadline.

## Attendance
- Attendance details are updated on the student dashboard every week.
- Contact your class advisor for discrepancy resolution.
