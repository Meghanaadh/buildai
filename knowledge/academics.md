# Academics at Vidyuth Institute of Technology (VIT), Hyderabad

## Academic Calendar Highlights
- **Odd Semester (2026):** August - December 2026
- **Even Semester (2027):** January - May 2027

## Departments Offered
VIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various specializations:
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- Civil Engineering (CE)
- Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Information Technology (IT)
- Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML)
- Cyber Security

## Grading System
VIT follows a **10-point CGPA scale**:
- **O (Outstanding):** 10 grade points (90-100 marks)
- **A+ (Excellent):** 9 grade points (80-89 marks)
- **A (Very Good):** 8 grade points (70-79 marks)
- **B+ (Good):** 7 grade points (60-69 marks)
- **B (Average):** 6 grade points (50-59 marks)
- **C (Pass):** 5 grade points (40-49 marks)
- **F (Fail):** 0 grade points (<40 marks)

## Attendance Policy
- **Minimum Requirement:** 75% attendance is strictly enforced for eligibility to write the end-semester exams.
- **Condonation:** Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may be allowed to appear for exams subject to the payment of a Rs. 500 condonation fine, along with a valid medical certificate or authorized leave.
- Students below 65% attendance will be detained.

## Internal Assessment Structure
Internal marks contribute significantly to the final grade:
- **Mid-Examinations:** 3 mid-exams per semester (best 2 out of 3 considered).
- **Assignments:** Regular submissions as prescribed by faculty.
- **Lab Internals:** Continuous evaluation based on observation books, records, and internal viva.

## Exam Schedule (Odd Semester 2026)
- **Mid-1 Exams:** September 15 - 20, 2026
- **Mid-2 Exams:** October 20 - 25, 2026
- **End-Semester Exams:** November 25 - December 10, 2026

## Supplementary Examinations
Supplementary exams are conducted twice a year alongside regular end-semester exams. Students with backlogs must register online via the student portal.

## MOOC / NPTEL Credit Transfer Policy
Students are encouraged to take MOOCs/NPTEL courses. Credits earned from approved courses can be transferred against department electives or open electives. Prior approval from the Head of Department is required.

## Faculty Office Hours & Timings
- **Faculty Office Hours:** Monday - Friday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- **Lab Timings:** Scheduled between 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM (varies by batch)
