# Academics

## Notes
- Department notes are uploaded weekly by faculty coordinators.
- Students should use the LMS folder mapped by semester and subject.

## Exam Schedule
- Mid-semester timetable is released two weeks before exams.
- Final examination schedule is published by the Controller of Examinations.

## Course Registration
- Course registration opens during the announced registration window.
- Late registration requires HoD approval.

## Attendance
- Attendance is published at the end of each week.
- Students below attendance threshold must contact their faculty advisor.

## Announcements
- Academic notices are posted on the student portal and department boards.
