# Administration at Vidyuth Institute of Technology (VIT), Hyderabad

## Office Information
- **College Timings:** 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM
- **Admin Office Hours:** 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (Monday - Saturday)
- **Contact:** admin@vidyuth.edu.in | +91-40-2345-6789

## Document Requests
- **Transcript Request Process:** Apply online via the campus ERP system. The processing fee is Rs. 200. Transcripts are generally issued within 3-5 working days.
- **Bonafide Certificate:** Can be requested from the administration desk. Certificates are issued on the same day if the application is submitted before 11:00 AM.
- **Transfer Certificate (TC):** Issued at the time of graduation or discontinuation. Requires clearance from all departments (Library, Hostel, Accounts, Labs).
- **Convocation:** Details regarding the annual convocation ceremony, including dress code and guest passes, are published on the ERP portal 2 months prior to the event.

## Fee Structure (Per Academic Year)
- **Tuition Fee:** Rs. 1,20,000 / year
- **Hostel Fee:** Rs. 60,000 / year (applicable for campus hostel residents)
- **Examination Fee:** Rs. 3,000 / semester

## Scholarships & Financial Aid
- **Merit-Based:** Awarded to the top 10% of students in each branch based on CGPA.
- **Category Waivers:** Partial or full fee waivers are available for SC/ST/OBC students as per government regulations. Submit valid caste and income certificates during admission/renewal.

## ID & Library Cards
- **ID Card Replacement:** In case of loss or damage, apply for a duplicate ID card at the admin office. A replacement fee of Rs. 150 applies.
- **Library Card:** Issued during orientation. Grants access to the central library and digital resources. Replacement fee for a lost library card is Rs. 100.
