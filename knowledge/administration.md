# Administration

## Transcript Process
- Submit transcript request through administration helpdesk form.
- Attach student ID details and semester information.
- Typical processing time is 3 to 5 working days.

## Bonafide Certificate Process
- Bonafide requests are accepted through administration office workflow.
- Students must provide purpose, department, and required date.
- Approved certificates are issued digitally or at administration desk.

## General Certificates
- Certificate requests should include accurate identity and program details.
- For urgent requests, contact administration office directly.
