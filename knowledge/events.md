# Events

## Upcoming Events
- College events are managed by student affairs and clubs.
- Official event dates are posted on the campus calendar.

## Hackathons
- Hackathon registrations are announced through event notices.
- Team size and eligibility depend on individual event rules.

## Workshops
- Workshop signups may have limited seats.
- Priority is based on registration timestamp when seats are limited.

## Clubs
- Club activities and meeting schedules are published weekly.
