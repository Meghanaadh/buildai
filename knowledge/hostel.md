# Hostel

## Nearby Hostels
- Nearby private hostels and contact details are maintained by hostel office.
- Students should verify rent and facility details directly before booking.

## Vacancies
- Vacancy lists are refreshed based on hostel partner updates.
- Availability may change without prior notice.

## Rules
- Students must follow quiet hours, visitor restrictions, and safety norms.
- Violations should be reported to wardens.

## Complaints
- Maintenance and infrastructure complaints can be filed through hostel complaint form.
- For urgent safety issues, contact warden and emergency desk immediately.
