# Placements

## Notifications
- Placement announcements are published by the placement cell.
- Eligibility criteria are listed for every company notice.

## Upcoming Companies
- Company visit schedules are shared on the placement dashboard.
- Students must verify eligibility before applying.

## Resume Tips
- Keep resume to one page unless instructed otherwise.
- Highlight internships, projects, and measurable achievements.

## Mock Interview Registration
- Mock interview registration opens through placement forms.
- Confirmation is sent after slot allocation.

## Guidance
- Students can book mentoring support through placement office hours.
