# Support

## Mental Wellness
- Students can contact counseling support for stress, anxiety, or emotional concerns.
- Wellness support is confidential within institutional policy limits.

## Counseling
- Counseling appointments can be requested through student support desk.
- Students may ask for one-on-one or group support sessions.

## Anti-Ragging
- Anti-ragging complaints should be reported immediately through official channels.
- Safety concerns must be escalated without delay.

## Emergency Contacts
- Campus emergency support is available through the institutional helpline.
- In immediate danger, contact local emergency services and nearest campus authority.
