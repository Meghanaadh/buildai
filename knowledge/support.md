# Student Support

## Mental Wellness
- Students experiencing stress or anxiety can access counseling support.
- Short guided wellbeing sessions are available through Student Support.

## Counseling Appointment
- Counseling appointments can be requested from the Student Support page.

## Anti-Ragging
- Anti-ragging reports are treated as high-priority grievances.
- Immediate help is available through emergency support contacts.

## Emergency Contacts
- Use campus emergency contacts for urgent medical or safety concerns.
